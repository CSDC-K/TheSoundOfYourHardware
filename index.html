The Sound Of Your Hardware - Documentation

1. Adding Custom Sounds

You can personalize your hardware orchestra by adding your own audio files. Drop your .mp3 files directly into the application's local data directory inside the Sounds/ folder. Use the refresh button next to the sound selector to load them instantly.

2. Setting Up Automated Triggers

This app works like an acoustic automation system. You define the thresholds, and the hardware sings accordingly:

  • Target App: Click the list icon to bind the trigger engine to a specific active process PID.
  • Hardware Limits: Adjust the sliders for CPU, GPU, RAM usage, and core heat limits. When your system crosses these markers, your selected sound will trigger.

3. Fine-Tuning & SoundFX

Open the Adjustments panel to manipulate the active audio pipeline:

  • Sound Level: Control the output master volume. Pushing past 100% unleashes the full potential of your system's voice.
  • Check Interval: Sets how frequently (in seconds) the background backend polls your hardware state.
  • SoundFX List: Select real-time playback speed manipulation (up to 3.0x) to make the tone sound panicked as your hardware hits critical stress levels.
